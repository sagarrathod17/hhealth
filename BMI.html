BMI (Overview):

A body mass index is referred to as a measurement of leanness or corpulence that dervived from the mass (weight) and height of a person. Remember that this number does not measure body fat directly, however, it is moderately correlated with more direct measures of body fat. It is defined as body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is universally expressed in units of kg/m^2 , resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres. BMI calculation is considered to determine whether a person is underweight, healthy weight, overweight or obese. Remember that bmi ranges may vary depends on your exercise regime. The more you gain weight more will be the body mass index, but it doesn’t mean that you are obese it may be because of high muscle also.

What is BMI Formula?

1. English BMI Formula :

BMI = weight(lb)/(Height(in))² * 703
When you considering English measurements or the above bmi equation, pounds should be divided by inches squared. And then, this should be multiplied by 703 to convert from lbs/inches² kg/m².

2. Metric BMI Formula :

BMI = Weight(kg) / Height(m)²
The above equation is demonstrated weight in kg divided by height in meters squared. If your height has measured in centimeters (cm), then all you need to divide it by 100 to convert this to meters (m).

How is BMI Interpreted For Adults?

For adults 20 years old and older, Body Mass Index is interpreted by using standard weight status categories. Experts depicted that the bmi scale is something that will place you in one of six categories. These categories for BMI are the same for adult men and women of all body types and ages. Now, look at the given chart!
Classification Body Mass Index Score
Underweight less than 18.5
Normal Weight 18.5 – 24.9
Overweight 25 – 29.9
Obesity (Class 1) 30 – 34.9
Obesity (Class 2) 35 – 39.9
Extreme Obesity (Class 3) 40+

BMI less than 18.5 (Underweight):

A lean Body Mass Index can refer to as your weight maybe too low. Remember that the lower your body mass range the greater the health risks become!

BMI is between 18.5 and 24.9 (Normal Weight or Healthy Range):

Remember that individuals who have a BMI between 18.5 to 24.9 possess the ideal amount of body weight.

BMI is between 25 and 29.9 (Overweight):

Remember that such an overweight range increased the risk for a variety of illnesses. Recent studies found an increased rate of diabetes, blood pressure, and heart disease was recorded at 27.8 for men and 27.3 for women. So, try the above body mass index calculator for women and men to find the your normal range! Also, once you know you are overweight, consult to your health care provider!

BMI is between 30-34.99 (Obese Class 1):

People within this range are prone to a physically unhealthy condition. Ideally, to reach bmi healthy range, you ought to consult your health care provider and consider reducing your weight by 5% to 10%. This weight reduction is good to reach your ideal bmi for adult men and women.

BMI is between 35-39.99 (Obese Class 2):

If you fall within this range, then experts depict that the risk of weight-related health problems and even death, is severe. So, immediately consult your MEDICAL PRO for optimal weight range.

BMI is over 40 (Obese Class 3 : Morbid Obesity):

Studies depicted that if an individual have BMI of 40+, it indicates that he/she have an extremely high risk of weight-related disease and even premature death. So, consult to Doctor to get recommendations according to your body mass index ranges!
