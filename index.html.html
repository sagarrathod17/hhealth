About Health :

‘Health is Wealth’ it is a well-known term! No doubt, health plays a vital role in the life cycle of humans. Every individual across the globe wants to remain fit and healthy. Both physically and mentally fitness and health are essential to become a successful person in your personal as well as professional life. Optimistic studies show that there are lots of people who are battling various diseases as they can’t maintain their fitness and health on a daily basis. But, quit worrying! We are providing simple and tested health calculators that monitor your several health issues. You can examine your daily health status with the ease of a simple health status calculator. The fitness calculator has its own scope and limitations, so before utilizing the healthy calculator, you ought to consult with your health care provider!
Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) Calculator - Calculates the approximate blood alcohol content of the user, based on user's gender, amount of alcohol consumed, weight, and time spent drinking.
Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator – Calculates the proportion of the user's weight to height. Based on federal guidelines and can be used to assess possible existing or future health risks
Calculate Your BMR – Calculates basal metabolic rate or the number of calories burned while exerting absolutely minimal energy. The BMR also indicates the minimum number of calories needed to perform essential bodily functions.
Calculate Your Body Composition – Determines the user's military body fat, based on the Circumference Method. Requires a tape measure in addition to basic statistics, including height, weight, and body part measurement. Determines the user's body fat percentage using a skin fold caliper and three data points. Calculations are based on the Jackson/Pollock 3-Site Caliper Method. Requires caliper and data input.
Heart Rate Calculator – Calculates a range of possible heart rates, including resting heart rate, maximum heart rate, reserve heart rate, target heart rate, and heart rate training zones.
Hypocalcemia (Calcium) Calculator – Assesses the presence of low serum calcium levels in the blood. While most calcium in the human body is in bones, this small, closely regulated percentage is in blood.
Carboplatin AUC Calculator – Calculates dosage for a range of chemotherapy drugs primarily used to treat cancers of the head, neck, endometrial, esophageal, breast, bladder, cervical, and central nervous system.
Body Mass Index – A simple calculator that determines the user's body mass index. Simply requires height and weight to produce the calculation.
Weight Calorie Calculator – Calculates the daily calories that a user must consume in order to meet and maintain a desired weight. Data inputs include height, weight, body mass, activity level, and desired weight.
Waist to Hip Ratio Calculator – Calculates the user's waist to hip ratio and determines body type. The device shows the results in conjunction with any health risks to which the user may be subject.
APACHE II Calculator – Determines APACHE II, or Acute Physiology and Chronic Health Evaluation II, a classification system for disease severity. This is typically applied to a patient upon entering an intensive care unit.
